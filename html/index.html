About

My name is David O’Brien and I am a Southern California based Web Developer and Designer. I specialize in creating stunning web pages, effective email marketing campaigns, and eye catching banners. I have been working as a designer for 5 years now and I really enjoy being able to be creative everyday. I am a graduate of The Art Institute of California with a Bachelor of Science in Game Art & Design.

While working at VersaProducts, I expanded my knowledge in the field and I enjoyed the opportunity to try new designs and learn more about development. I work with the marketing team to create emails, landing pages, and other campaign images to entice new and existing customers to buy our products.

In my free time, I enjoy playing dodgeball, managing a dodgeball league, going to the beach, playing video games, and working out at the gym.
