Landing Page Email Campaign Images

Overview

The Client VersaDesk is an electric standing desk brand under VersaProducts that focuses on promoting health in the workplace. The original push-button electric sit-to-stand desk riser and leader in standing desk solutions specializing in innovative designs since 2000. VersaDesk is committed to manufacturing the kind of ergonomic, user-friendly home and office furniture that will last a lifetime.

The Challenge Design and develop a marketing campaign for the New Year - Flash Sale to promote the Sunrise Desk which includes creating an email, landing page, hero image, top strip banner, and google display ads.

Target Persona VersaDesk is targeted toward a customer that is looking for a standing desk at a reasonable price, that doesn't have to be the best on the market. The Father's Day Sale offers a deal that allows the customer to easily make a purchase of a standing desk.

Outcome A campaign design for VersaDesk that is unique featuring new designs. In this campaign, I used a lot of gradient colors to make the designs pop.
