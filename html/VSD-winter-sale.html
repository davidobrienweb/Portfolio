Winter Holiday Sale

Landing Page Email Campaign Images

Overview

The Client VersaDesk is an electric standing desk brand under VersaProducts that focuses on promoting health in the workplace. The original push-button electric sit-to-stand desk riser and leader in standing desk solutions specializing in innovative designs since 2000.

The Challenge VersaDesk needed potential and recurring customers to be made better aware of their sale promotions, as they had not been receiving as much traction as desired. They needed a strategic and themed marketing campaign that would promote all of their winter-specific deals to help close out their Q4 quotas.

Target Persona VersaDesk was targeted toward customers that were looking for a standing desk at a reasonable price. They work in the office or at home and may have experienced back or neck pain in the past which has resulted in their interest in purchasing a standing desk.

Outcome To drive interactions with the VersaDesk product line, I designed and developed a marketing campaign for the Winter Holiday Sale that promoted the brand and drove sales. I generated a new email, landing page, hero image, top strip banner, and google display ads that uniquely featured the new designs promoting the winter sale. In this campaign, I used GIFs animations that brought the design to life and guided the attention of the viewer to key sales points. I used a color palette of red, white, and light blue for the color scheme because they are associated with winter, Christmas, and Hanukkah. The creative materials I generated for this page, ranging from landing pages to banner ads to responsive emails, all had a cohesive look and fun, festive design that makes people want to be a part of VersaDesk. I developed the landing page and emails by generating modern HTML and CSS code using Flexbox that is responsive to all devices, including different smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

Role Designer / Developer

Landing Page

I used animated snow to give the design a winter feel and bring the landing page to life. In the product section, I highlighted the features of each desk such as the eclectic touch button or an adjustable keyboard that animates when a user scrolls to that section. Below the product page was a thumbnail image of a video promoting the product that users can watch to learn more. Lastly, an animation of a VersaDesk desk rising and lowering showcases how the desk functions.
