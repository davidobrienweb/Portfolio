USA Dodgeball

Sports Community App

Overview Final Product
Overview

The Client USA Dodgeball is an organization that promotes and develops the sport of dodgeball across the United States; they host tournaments across the country and organize the national team representing the United States in the World Dodgeball Federation to play against teams from around the world. USA Dodgeball aims to unite the nation under a governing body that gives a voice to many organizations who share the goal of developing and promoting the sport of dodgeball.

The Challenge USA Dodgeball wanted a more efficient and better organized way for players to get information about dodgeball events and help make it easier to find other players and leagues. While they already have Facebook for hosting events and their website for getting other information to players, they wanted a platform that has all the information about dodgeball a player would need in one place.

The Process To get the app design started, I looked over the information currently provided on the USA Dodgeball website and Facebook profile, making a list of the items it lacked and what was useful to include in the app, making it serviceable for both the players and organizers. I presented my ideas to USA Dodgeball players and learned more about their current frustrations with the website and Facebook, as well as what they would look for in a dodgeball app to convince them to download and utilize it regularly. After conducting research in the dodgeball community, I generated the user flow chart based on the average player’s needs, and from there I created multiple wireframe options. I then utilized Adobe XD to generate mock ups of the different pages within the app to create a working prototype.

Outcome The final design of the USA Dodgeball app is a one stop resource for a player to utilize when looking to join a new team or register for a tournament. From the premier league section that has the upcoming tournament information and ability to find a free agent player to shopping for USA Dodgeball merchandise, I designed an app that resolves the current frustrations of the USA Dodgeball members. I categorized the sections into 5 key areas: premier league, Team USA, member organizations, shop, and media.

Home Screen
When the user opens the app, the home screen displays all of the categories available. A cart icon in the top right allows users to easily access their shopping cart. In the top left, I included a sidebar navigation button that displays everything available in the app for quick navigation. From the sidebar navigation, users can log in or register for USA Dodgeball. Once signed in, users can view the tournaments they have favorited and edit their free agent post. Users can also find a link to the recent announcements from USA Dodgeball in the header section, where it is easily viewed when there is a new announcement available.

Premier League
The most used feature of the app, the Premier League section houses all tournaments that USA Dodgeball hosts as well as a quick way to register for them. The Teams section displays all current teams that play in the Premier League. Users can see the players on each team as well as links to the team’s social media accounts. The Free Agents section allows players who don’t have a team to create a profile and describe how they can be an asset to any potential teams. Other players can view who posted in the Free Agent section to find a new member for their team. The Rules section displays the updated rules for USA Dodgeball which can also be downloaded from the app.

Team USA
In the Team USA section, users can find: a link to the shop section to browse official Team USA Dodgeball gear, the selection process for Team USA, the results from past years, the previous rosters of the team, the World Dodgeball Federation, and the coaching staff for USA Dodgeball. Most importantly, users can sign up to be considered for Team USA.

Member Organizations
In the Member Organizations section, users can search for other dodgeball leagues that are located all over the country under USA Dodgeball. Players would find this useful when they are traveling and would like to play in local leagues of the city they are visiting or to find a league in the city they reside in.

Shop
The Shop section contains all the merchandise that users can purchase including jerseys, hoodies, baby bibs, hats, and dodgeballs. Users can easily swap through categories when browsing using the icons at the top of the page making it easier to shop. In the product page, users can view images of the product and select their size, color preference, and quantity. The shopping cart handles all of the purchases that can be made on the app such as registration for a tournament, signing up for USA Dodgeball membership, and shopping for merchandise. The shopping cart is streamlined for an easy buying experience by only having the user input their shipping and billing information that can be saved once a profile is created.

Media
Users can view the various photos, videos, and news coverage of dodgeball in the media section. The photos are broken up into albums and when viewing the photos the user can easily scroll through the imagery. The videos can be played in the app and all the news coverage of dodgeball links to outside sites.
